Frequently Asked Questions

What is the user base of plasm-O-pheresis?

The user base of plasm-O-pheresis are the hospitals and healthcare centres that provide plasma treatment to Covid-19 patients.

What are the future aspects of plasm-O-pheresis?

In the future, plasm-O-pheresis can be used as a portal to track ambulances and also as a portal that will keep track of the covid vaccine distribution in various areas.

Why should hospitals choose plasm-O-pheresis?

plasm-O-pheresis is a platform that will maintain hospital's database for them. It will keep track of the patients that are willing to donate their plasma for this good deed and will show only those patients that are ready to donate their plasma after finishing their 14 day period.
